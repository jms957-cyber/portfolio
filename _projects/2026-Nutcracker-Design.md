Problem Statement: Determine the size of a Nutcracker based on your own choice of nut size and given/applied strength. 

Given: Height of the nut, average human strength/grip, typical standarized lengths, breaking force of nut.

Approach: I chose to go with a ratio comparison between the front of the nutcracker and the end. As the height of the nut is given, as is a standarized distance from the front to the cracking component, we can set up a ratio of front height/distance and back height/distance. To get to this point, we first must consider the ratio of force required to distance of contact. To determine the distance of the back end, we can use the breaking force of the nut and given human strength with the given front distance. 

Discussion: After going through these calculations, I determined the end length to be over 20cm, which seems within reason but borderline impractical. An item of such niche capabilities would be worth it if it is smalll and easy to store, rather than being of such abnormal length.
